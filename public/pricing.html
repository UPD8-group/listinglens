Common questions

Why 2 reports for $1?
We want you to see the full product and come back for more. Your $1 gets you 2 complete analyst reports with no restrictions — score, all flags with reasoning, negotiation anchor, ownership costs, market comparisons, questions to ask, and pre-purchase checklist. Use one, and you've still got a credit in the bank for next time. At 50 cents a report, it's a no-brainer.
Does input quality affect the report?
Yes. URLs give the most data and produce the richest reports. Screenshots and pasted text are next best. A quick photo will still generate a useful report, but with fewer data points. We show a completeness indicator so you always know. Pro users can combine multiple inputs for deeper analysis.
How accurate are the estimates?
Where data comes from verified sources (land registries, council records), we flag it as verified. Where the AI is estimating (insurance, renovation costs, depreciation), it's clearly marked. Reports are buyer intelligence, not formal valuations — always conduct your own due diligence.
What categories do you support?
At launch: Property, Vehicles, Electronics, and General (furniture, fashion, collectibles, marketplace items). The AI adapts its scoring dimensions, flag types, and cost sections to each category automatically. More categories coming based on demand.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans cancel instantly. Annual plans can be cancelled and will run until the end of the billing period. Pay-as-you-go credit packs never expire. No lock-in contracts.
Do unused reports roll over?
No. Monthly report allowances reset each billing cycle. If you only need occasional reports, pay-as-you-go credit packs are better value and never expire.
Is my data shared?
No. Your reports are saved to your dashboard for your reference only. We don't sell data, share search history, or use your queries for advertising. Your report history is yours.
